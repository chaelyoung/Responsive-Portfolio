About Me

MICHAEL YANG is a filmmaker, graphic artist, and photographer based in the Minneapolis-Saint Paul area in Minnesota. When he isn't working, Yang spends his time reading, writing, and staying physically active. He is currently a coding student, enrolled in the UMN Coding Bootcamp program. His past work includes film productions, branding, and organizational development. Yang has a profound passion for visual media and tailors his work to uniquely convey the experience and message of his clients.
